My background in both development and operations allows me to offer perspective and a wealth of experience to my clients. My expertise lies in ensuring the reliability, availability, and scalability of complex software systems.

I pride myself on continuously learning and adapting to new technologies, ensuring that I can provide the best solutions and services to meet your ever-evolving needs.
